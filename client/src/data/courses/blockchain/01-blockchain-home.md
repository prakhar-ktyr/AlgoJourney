# Blockchain Tutorial

Welcome to the **Blockchain Tutorial** — your complete guide to understanding blockchain technology from fundamentals to building decentralized applications.

---

## What You'll Learn

- **Fundamentals** — How blockchain works, cryptography, consensus
- **Bitcoin** — Transactions, mining, wallets, scripting
- **Ethereum** — Smart contracts, Solidity programming, EVM
- **Smart Contract Development** — Testing, security, token standards
- **DeFi** — DEXs, lending, stablecoins, flash loans
- **Advanced Topics** — Layer 2, ZK proofs, DAOs, interoperability
- **Building DApps** — Web3 development, frontend integration

---

## Who Is This For?

- Complete beginners curious about blockchain
- Developers wanting to build decentralized applications
- Finance professionals exploring DeFi
- Anyone interested in Web3 technology

---

## Prerequisites

- Basic computer literacy
- Some programming knowledge helps (JavaScript recommended)
- No blockchain experience needed

---

## Why Blockchain Matters

Blockchain is transforming industries:

- **Finance** — $100B+ locked in DeFi protocols
- **Supply Chain** — Transparent tracking from origin to consumer
- **Healthcare** — Secure, interoperable medical records
- **Gaming** — True ownership of in-game assets (NFTs)
- **Identity** — Self-sovereign digital identity
- **Governance** — Transparent voting and DAOs
