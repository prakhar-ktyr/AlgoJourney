# Cybersecurity Tutorial

Welcome to the **Cybersecurity Tutorial** — your complete guide to understanding and practicing cybersecurity from beginner to advanced.

---

## What You'll Learn

This course covers everything you need to know about cybersecurity:

- **Fundamentals** — Threats, attacks, and defense principles
- **Network Security** — Firewalls, VPNs, intrusion detection
- **Web Application Security** — OWASP Top 10, XSS, SQL injection
- **Cryptography** — Encryption, hashing, digital certificates
- **Identity & Access** — Authentication, MFA, Zero Trust
- **Cloud & System Security** — Containers, SIEM, vulnerability management
- **Advanced Topics** — Pen testing, incident response, compliance
- **Capstone Project** — Real-world security audit

---

## Who Is This For?

- Complete beginners curious about cybersecurity
- Developers wanting to write more secure code
- IT professionals transitioning to security roles
- Students preparing for security certifications

---

## Prerequisites

- Basic computer literacy
- Familiarity with networking concepts is helpful but not required
- No programming experience needed (though it helps for later topics)

---

## Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cyber attacks cost the global economy **trillions of dollars** annually. Every organization needs security professionals to protect their data, systems, and users.

Key facts:
- A cyber attack occurs every **39 seconds** on average
- The average data breach costs **$4.45 million**
- There are **3.5 million unfilled** cybersecurity jobs worldwide
- **95%** of breaches are caused by human error
